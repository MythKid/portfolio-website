A little about me

Who I am, what I am studying, and where I sit in the Cyber Security and Networking space at Unitec.

I am a third year Bachelor of Computing Systems student at Unitec Institute of Technology in Auckland, specializing in Cyber Security and Networking. My focus is on building secure, resilient network environments and growing toward a career in defensive security.

Alongside my studies I stay active in the student community. I work as a PASS Leader supporting other students, represent the student body as an International Representative on the Student Council, and was selected as a Student Ambassador for Unitec in 2025.

These roles have sharpened my communication, leadership, and professionalism, qualities I bring into every technical project I take on.
